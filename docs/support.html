❓ Frequently asked questions

How do I connect to my TV?

Make sure your iPhone and TV are on the same Wi‑Fi network. Open the app, tap Connect your device, then use the AirPlay button to choose your TV.

How does screen mirroring work?

You can mirror in two ways: use Wi‑Fi TV browser mirror inside the app (open the link on your smart TV browser), or use AirPlay Screen Mirroring from iOS Control Center for Apple TV and compatible TVs.

Why is my TV not listed?

Confirm both devices are on the same Wi‑Fi, AirPlay is enabled on your TV, and your TV supports AirPlay or screen mirroring. Restart your router and TV if needed.

How do I cast photos or videos?

Go to Photo or Video in the app, select a file from your library, then tap the AirPlay icon and choose your TV.

How do I change language or dark mode?

Open Settings in the app. You can switch language (English, French, Spanish, Arabic) and appearance (System, Light, or Dark).
